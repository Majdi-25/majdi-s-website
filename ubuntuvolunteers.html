Become a Volunteer

UbuntuPath volunteers help grow the movement by engaging communities, collecting signatures, recruiting members, and assisting with fundraising. If you believe in unity, service, and change — we welcome you.

Volunteer Declaration:
Volunteers act in a voluntary, unpaid capacity and may not represent themselves as official spokespeople or handle funds unless expressly authorised.

Any donations collected must be transferred directly to official UbuntuPath accounts. Volunteers may not retain cash or promise benefits of any kind.

By submitting this form, you confirm that the information provided is accurate and that you agree to act ethically, lawfully, and in the best interests of UbuntuPath.
