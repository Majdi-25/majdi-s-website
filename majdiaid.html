MajdiAid

After relocating to Cape Town, Majdi (I.D. Williams) was exposed to financial disadvantage, both personally and to that of the communities, of Cape Town. Although there are many groups assisting, the needs of individuals and communities are not addressed holistically. These experiences and the lack of holistic assistance, motivated the creation of MajdiAid by Majdi.

This motivaton and the name Majdi (which is of Arabc origin and loosely translated means Noble/Honourable) align perfectly, with the two core values of the platform...
1. Addressing the individual and community holistically (physically, mentally, emotionally, spiritually and financially), with NOBLE intent.
2. Ensuring every interaction brings HONOR, to the individual and community.

The mission at MajdiAid is simple...
1. To make a real and permanent impact, uplifting individuals and communities holistically.
2. Creating a charitable (pay-it-forward) network, dedicated to inspiring, supportng and uplifting individuals and communities holistically.

In an effort to achieve these objectives, MajdiAid welcomes individuals, communities, businesses, NGO's, donors, government and political agencies (ANYONE) to work wth us, for the betterment of all individuals and communities.
